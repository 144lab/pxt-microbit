Create an animation with a frame displaying a smiley face and the next frame with no LEDs lit up. This will make it look like the smiley face is blinking as the display switches between frames.

```
basic.showAnimation(`
. # . # . . . . . .
. # . # . . . . . .
. . . . . . . . . .
# . . . # . . . . .
. # # # . . . . . .
`, 400) // ***
```
